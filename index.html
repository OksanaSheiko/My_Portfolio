About me

❖I'm specializing in manual testing of web and mobile applications. With 1.5 years of hands-on experience, I’ve come to realize one thing: testing is much more than just a profession — it’s a way to be part of something meaningful.

❖I love exploring weak spots in a product because I genuinely care about how the end user will experience it. I want things to work smoothly, reliably, and intuitively. For me, it’s about respecting people and their time.

❖Every day, I explore application logic, work with test cases, expand scenarios, run regression tests, monitor quality, write documentation, and share knowledge with my colleagues. Being part of a team matters to me — where we test and actively improve the product together, support one another, share experience, and move toward common goals.

❖I’m learning test automation, earning the basics of Java Script and Python and test automation using it. Because I see its potential to make our work faster, deeper, and more scalable. I want to combine the creativity of manual testing with the precision of automation to deliver truly high-quality software.

❖I believe a QA engineer — it’s someone who understands how things should work and helps bring that vision to life. That’s why I’m here. And that’s why I love what I do.
